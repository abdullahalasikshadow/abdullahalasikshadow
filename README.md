<table>

<td width="60%">

**🌟 ASIK AI — Free Personal AI Assistant**

A completely free AI assistant for students and developers. Powered by **Groq** (blazing fast) and **Google Gemini** (super smart). Study, code, solve math, write essays — anything. 14,400 requests/day, zero cost, zero signup.

**Tech Stack:**
`HTML` `Tailwind CSS` `Vanilla JS` `Groq API` `Gemini API` `GitHub Pages`

**Why it's special:**
- 100% Free Forever — no credit card
- Privacy first — API keys stay in your browser
- Mobile ready — works on phone & desktop
- No backend — maximum privacy
